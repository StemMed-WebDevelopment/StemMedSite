Fueling the Future of STEM and Medicine Where It’s Needed Most

STEM-med is a youth-led nonprofit expanding access to science, research, and medical opportunity in underserved communities — one initiative at a time.

Explore Our Work

About Us

Founded by Roman Baptiste, STEM-med connects underserved students with scientific opportunities, mentorship, and medical insight through community-rooted action.

"We don’t wait for opportunity. We build it." – Roman B.

Initiatives

Our current initiative is addressing the healthcare gap in rural communities through raising support and awareness.

  • Providing Rural Health Support: Providing education and resources to improve healthcare access in rural Illinois.
  • Building Community Health Workshops: Bringing hands-on health education directly to local schools and centers.
  • Volunteer Support: Connecting youth volunteers with local clinics to assist and learn.
