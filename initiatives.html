Rural Healthcare Initiative

At STEM-med, our Rural Healthcare Initiative focuses on bridging the gap in medical access for underserved rural communities, beginning with Vermilion County, Illinois.

Our Mission

We strive to improve healthcare education, accessibility, and community engagement in rural areas where medical resources are scarce and healthcare disparities run deep.

Current Goal

Our current goal is to contribute to addressing the opioid crisis in Vermilion County.

  • Raise Community Awareness: We will run a social media campaign to attempt to educate masses on what you can personally do to contribute.
  • Build Local Partnerships: Connect with healthcare providers, clinics, and organizations to collaborate on improving access to life saving remedys like Narcan.
  • Gather and Share Data: Collect local health statistics and community insights to inform and direct action.
  • Distribute Narcan: Broaden access to the life saving medication Narcan that reverses opioids like heroin, fentanyl, and oxycodone.

Get Involved

Your support can make a real impact. Whether through volunteering, donations, or spreading awareness, you can help us drive change in rural healthcare.
