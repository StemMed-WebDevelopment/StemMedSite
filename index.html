Fueling the Future of STEM and Medicine

STEM-med is a youth-led nonprofit expanding access to science, research, and medical opportunity in underserved communities, one initiative at a time.

Join Our Mission

STEM-Med was founded by Roman Baptiste and is currently in its early stages as a student-led initiative. We're looking for passionate individuals who want to help expand access to healthcare and STEM education in underserved communities.

Initiatives

Our current initiative is addressing the healthcare gap in rural healthcare through raising support for it.

  • Providing Rural Health Support: Education and resources to improve healthcare access in rural Illinois.
  • Building Community Health Workshops: Bringing hands-on health education directly to local schools and centers.
  • Volunteer Support: Connecting youth volunteers with local clinics to assist and learn.
