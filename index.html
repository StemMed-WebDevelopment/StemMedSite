Fueling the Future of STEM and Medicine

STEM-med is a youth-led nonprofit expanding access to science, research, and medical opportunity in underserved communities, one initiative at a time.

Join Our Mission

STEM-Med was founded by Roman Baptiste and is currently in its early stages as a student-led initiative. We're looking for passionate individuals who want to help expand access to healthcare and STEM education in underserved communities.

Initiatives

Our current goal is addressing the opiod crises in Vermilion Country.

  • Supplying Narcan: We hope through fiscal sponsorship we can apply for grants and Narcan shipping services.
  • Supplying Fentanyl Test Strips: We hope to supply Fentanyl test strips to people in underserved areas..
  • Distribute Supplies: With these supplies we hop to distribute them to underserved, and vital shelters throughout Vermilion county.
