Rural Healthcare Initiative

At STEM-med, our Rural Healthcare Initiative focuses on bridging the gap in medical access for underserved rural communities, beginning with Vermilion County, Illinois.

Our Mission

We strive to improve healthcare education, accessibility, and community engagement in rural areas where medical resources are scarce and healthcare disparities run deep.

Current Goals

We’re actively laying the foundation to address rural healthcare disparities through focused goals that guide our efforts today and shape our future impact.

  • Raise Community Awareness: Inform rural residents about healthcare challenges and preventive health through targeted outreach.
  • Build Local Partnerships: Connect with healthcare providers, clinics, and organizations to collaborate on improving access.
  • Gather and Share Data: Collect local health statistics and community insights to inform and direct action.
  • Develop Educational Materials: Create accessible health and STEM resources tailored to rural communities.
  • Establish Volunteer and Mentorship Opportunities: Link students and residents with healthcare professionals for hands-on learning and support.

Get Involved

Your support can make a real impact. Whether through volunteering, donations, or spreading awareness, you can help us drive change in rural healthcare.
