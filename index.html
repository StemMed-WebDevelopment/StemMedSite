Fueling the Future of STEM and Medicine Where It’s Needed Most

STEM-med is a youth-led nonprofit expanding access to science, research, and medical opportunity in underserved communities — one initiative at a time.

Explore Our Work

About Us

Founded by Roman Baptiste, STEM-med connects underserved students with scientific opportunities, mentorship, and medical insight through community-rooted action.

"We don’t wait for opportunity. We build it." – Roman B.

STEM-med Research

We drive cutting-edge youth-led projects tackling real-world challenges in medicine and science — from community health data analysis to innovative STEM education models.

  • Project Insight: Mapping rural healthcare disparities using data science tools.
  • Innovation Lab: Designing low-cost diagnostic devices for underserved clinics.

Initiatives

Our active programs designed to empower underserved students and build STEM pathways.

  • Mentorship Program: Pairing students with STEM professionals for guidance and skill-building.
  • Community Workshops: Hands-on science and health workshops held in local schools and centers.
  • Research Internships: Opportunities for high school students to participate in real lab work.
